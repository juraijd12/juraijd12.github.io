House Note

Counter seats release first on Mondays. Salon tables follow after confirmed pairings and private requests are placed.

Tasting

The menu moves in acts, not sections.

Opening bites are brighter and quicker. The middle deepens into smoke, stock, and reduction. Dessert finishes with bitterness, not sugar rush.
